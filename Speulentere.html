Speulentere spelle

With this app you can learn the maastrichts dialect. Go through the courses by playing different minigames and become one of the top speakers!

I created this app at Goal043 Serious Games. I did my internship at this company for half a year, and the team consisted of two developers and two game artists. During this internship, I also got to test the game, where it turned out that I have a talent for testing.
