Kings and pigs

This game is about reaching the end with a high score. You can score points by defeating enemies, collecting diamonds and hearts, and breaking crates. You can use the WASD keys to move and the space button to jump. You can attack with the X Key.

I made this game on my own. During the process, I learned how to add sound effects and to link it to the player. I also learned how to get something from a scene and use it in another scene while playing.
