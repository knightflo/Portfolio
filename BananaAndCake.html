Banana and Cake game

In this game, you must give either a banana or a cake to the customer on time! But first, you need to buy the item. To do that, you'll need money. Catch the floating money floating around the map, then go to your laptop to purchase the item!

I made this game by myself as a school project. Originally, it was supposed to be a gas station simulator, but due to limited time, I had to change the theme to something more manageable. During the development of this game, I learned a lot about using ScriptableObjects.
