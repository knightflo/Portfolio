Pong

In this game you defend your goal by hitting the ball to the opposite side. When the ball hits the opposite goal, you score a point. The first player with 10 points, wins. This game can be played in several modes.

This 2D game was my introduction to Unity and C# and made by myself. It was the first game that I made at school. During the process I learned a lot about perseverance: Finding a solution can take time.
