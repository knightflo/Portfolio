Pong

You defend your goal by hitting the ball to the opposite side. When the ball hits the opposite goal, you score a point. The first player with 10 points, wins. This game can be played in several modes.

This 2D game was my introduction to Unity and C# and made by myself. During the process I found out that finding a solution can take time. I learned a lot about perseverance. Finding a solution can take time.
