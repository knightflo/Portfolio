My first portfolio website

To be admitted to Sint Lucas, I introduced myself with this portfolio website which contains my projects before I joined Sint Lucas in september 2022. There are games in different program languages.

One game (flappy bob) I made as part of a programming course which I took in my spare time in january 2019. Other games I made with guidance of a programmer in my hometown. This was the first time I made a portfolio and I learned how to place my different projects on a website and how to define the layout. Also, I have though about my own logo. In flappy bob I learned to work with a counter and in another game, called Troll world, I learned how to make 3D world.
