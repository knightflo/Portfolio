Nekomancer

This game is about leading the cat to the end of the level. But there are enemies blocking the path! Use the WASD keys to move and the X button to attack. Use left shift to dash. If you find an NPC, you can use the E button to interact with him/her.

I made this game with a group of people. The group consisted of two developers and five Game art students. I have learned to align with what is achievable with a group in 36 hours. My contribution to this game was creating the dialogue And creating the start screen. I also created the map of the levels and the pause screen.
