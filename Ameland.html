Ameland

This website explains what island Ameland is and what you can do on the island. I have also made links to websites already existing for example: Wagenborg.nl (ferries) and tourist information.

I made this website as part of the Shine project for Sint lucas in the first year. I made it on my own but asked my teacher for help, especially for help with the navigation bar. During the process I learned how to place the pictures next to each other. I also spent a lot of time in styling.
