5 luik

I created this project for the Personality class at school. We had to visualise the 5 themes: pleasure, pride, individuality, growth and self-confidence. Here I represent the 5 projects which I created for the Personality class. Each project is representing one of the characteristics. Instead of showing 5 photos next to each other, I created a museum where you can walk through. In every room you can find one of the mentioned projects.

Creating the '5 luik' was an individual project, because it had to tell something about yourself. During the process, I discovered how to add animated pictures in a game.
